Welcome to Shree Coffee Haven

Every sip feels like you're in heaven. Experience authentic Nepali coffee and tea at Redwood Shopping Centre.

Our cozy environment, aromatic Nepali blends, and exceptional service create the perfect setting for you to relax and savor a true taste of Nepal. Whether you're a coffee enthusiast or someone who wants to experience a different culture, our café promises an unforgettable journey filled with warmth and community spirit.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to bringing the rich, authentic flavors of Nepali coffee and tea to Rotorua. Our mission goes beyond serving beverages; we aim to create a community space that celebrates Nepali culture, prioritizes sustainability, and offers an immersive cultural experience to our customers. From ethically sourced beans to eco-friendly practices, we’re committed to making a positive impact on both our community and the environment.
